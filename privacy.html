Brand: HÖRBI
Website: horbi.org
Partnership / contact: dluck.ru

For questions about this policy, use the contact form on the partnership page above.

2. What this Site does not do

  • We do not operate user accounts or a login area.
  • We do not run an online checkout on horbi.org.
  • Product purchases are completed on third-party shops (e.g. Wildberries) under their privacy policies.

3. Data we may process

3.1 Technical / server data

When you visit the Site, hosting providers and content delivery networks may automatically process technical data such as IP address, browser type, device type, date/time of access, and requested pages (server logs). This is required to deliver the Site securely and to diagnose errors.

3.2 Language preference

If you choose a language, we may store your preference in the browser (localStorage key horbi_lang) so the Site remembers it next time. This stays on your device unless you clear site data.

3.3 Messages you send us

If you contact us via the partnership form or email, we process the information you provide (name, email, message content) solely to respond and, where relevant, to discuss collaboration or wholesale.

3.4 Third-party services embedded or linked

  • Fonts — Google Fonts may load from Google servers when you open the Site.
  • Wildberries — product and brand links lead to wildberries.ru; their privacy rules apply there.
  • Partnership page — dluck.ru has its own privacy notice.

4. Purposes and legal bases

  • Provide and secure the Site — legitimate interest / necessity of technical processing.
  • Remember language — legitimate interest or your choice (preference stored locally).
  • Respond to inquiries — pre-contractual steps or legitimate interest in communication.
  • Comply with law — where we must retain or disclose data under applicable regulations.

If you are in the EEA/UK, the GDPR concepts of “legitimate interest”, “consent” (where used) and “contract” may apply as above.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

Details are in our separate Cookie Policy. In short: the Site itself does not require advertising cookies; technical storage may be used for language and host security.

6. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Processors that may receive technical data include website hosting/CDN providers. Shops and partners you choose to open are independent controllers of data you give them.

7. Retention

  • Server logs — typically short periods set by the host (often days to months).
  • Correspondence — as long as needed to handle the request and any related legal obligations.
  • Language preference — until you clear browser storage.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object, or port data, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise rights related to data we control for the Site, contact us via dluck.ru.

9. Children

The Site is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes

We may update this Policy. The “Last updated” date at the top will change. Continued use of the Site after updates means you acknowledge the revised text.
